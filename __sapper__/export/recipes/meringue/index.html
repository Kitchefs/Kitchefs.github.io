Serves: 18 Preparation Time: 15 Minutes Cook Time: 40 Minutes
Total Time: 1 Hour, 15 Minutes
Ingredients
Method
  1. Line baking tray with baking paper, preheat oven to 150°C
  2. Wisk egg whtes with eletric mixer until mixture holds.
  3. Wisk in sugar 1 tbsp at a time for about 5-10 minutes.
  4. Combine cornflour and vinegar in a seperate bowl then combine with egg mitxure.
  5. Divide mixture into 18 and spoon onto baking tray 3cm apart.*
  6. Twirl 1/2 tsp chocolate into each meringue.
  7. Decrease oven temperature to 120°C and bake for 35-40 minutes.*
  8. Turn off oven and do not open until completly cooled
Tips
  • You can turn this into a pavlova by placing mixture in a large circle and baking for 1 hour.
